About this book:

The work of the French American Painter Brigitte M. Wurm expresses the artists unique love of color, texture, and nature. In several ways, her creative process resembles how a gardener goes about her “Jardin” when working the soil and caring for her plants with bare hands or tools. Many among those admiring Brigitte’s art intuitively realize this uniqueness of her style and have been asking for a better access to Brigitte’s work. The four E-Artbooks “Flowering Art – The Paintings of Brigitte M. Wurm, Collected Works Part One to Part Four” will now hopefully provide this access Brigitte’s work. While her work can also be viewed at Brigitte’s online gallery , E-Artbooks allow the reader to explore the artist’s work at more leisure and in greater detail. Short of purchasing one of her paintings, these E-Artbooks are probably the best way to own some of Brigitte’s work. The present E-Artbook, “Collected Works - Part Two” of “Flowering Art – The Paintings of Brigitte M. Wurm” includes most of the artists paintings on square-shaped canvases and her pillar paintings.

For whom could this book be of interest?

Anyone seriously interested in modern art which calmly follows a different path from that of the louder mainstream modern art marketplace will enjoy perusing through the art world of this French-American artist. The artwork captured in this book is not screaming for attention but seeks to captivate viewers with its persistent creativity.
