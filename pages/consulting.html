New Consulting Projects

ATICE consulting builds on the decades-long career of its founder and owner at the leading edge of technology R&D in the semiconductor industry. As an owner-run company, ATICE it is a small business and the number of consulting projects it can commit to at any given time is limited. In addition, existing obligations to current or former clients may sometimes prevent ATICE from engaging with a prospective client or limit the scope of any consulting engagement.

Within the framework of those boundary conditions there are many areas and ways in which ATICE can deliver value to its clients. If your company is interesting in discussing a potential consulting project with ATICE we look forward to jointly explore if your company and ATICE can engage in such a project to the mutual benefit of both parties.

Industry Reports

From time to time, when for example a sufficiently large of number industry partners and/or clients express an interest in a certain topic which is more industry than client specific, ATICE will explore if it can meet this need with an industry report. ATICE will then make such reports available to all potential interested parties. If the interest in a topic should persist over a longer time among a substantial number of customers, ATICE may choose to update the respective reports as warranted.

Industry Market Data - Free Downloads

As a consulting business in the semiconductor industry ATICE keeps track of semiconductor market developments. A number of sources have been providing semiconductor market data to the public, free of charge, over many years. However, this is changing as companies now seek to monetize even basic industry data. Such general industry metrics, which ATICE just like others compiles for its internal use, is available for download on ATICE' website, free of charge. Even though provided in a format prepared by ATICE it is still public data and may come in handy for those active in this industry as well as for others following it for their own purposes.
