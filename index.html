Our Publishing Business

ATICE publishing offers authors help in crafting their own e-books by

  • supporting e-book creation in ePub 2.0 & 3.0 or PDF formats
  • offering the complete e-book creation process after reviewing an author's manuscript
  • assisting authors in their own efforts to create an e-book
  • publishing e-books through several major online e-book sales platforms

ATICE currently publishes e-books at Amazon, Apple, Google, and Kobo. Not every book can be published on all platforms as some may for example not handle formulas in mathematics books well or may have size limitations which exclude larger books.
E-books created by or with the support of ATICE can be published through ATICE. Authors may however also choose to publish their newly created e-books on their own.
